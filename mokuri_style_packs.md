# Mokuri Studio --- Style Pack Expansion Proposals

## Overview

This document outlines four style packs designed to expand Mokuri Studio
into a broader creative system. Each pack introduces a distinct
aesthetic lens while staying within the existing workflow.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Moribana Collection (Ikebana-Inspired Prints)

### Concept

Elegant still-life compositions inspired by Japanese flower arrangement.

### Key Characteristics

-   Minimal elements (3--7 per composition)
-   Strong use of negative space
-   Emphasis on balance and line

### Elements

-   Vessels (suiban, vases)
-   Branches (pine, plum, bamboo)
-   Flowers (iris, chrysanthemum, camellia)
-   Grasses and leaves

### Prompts

-   "A strong upward line"
-   "Balance in three elements"
-   "After the rain"

### Monetization

Premium pack (\$2.99--\$5.99)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Shin-hanga Light & Atmosphere

### Concept

Mood-driven scenes focused on light, weather, and atmosphere.

### Key Characteristics

-   Rain, snow, fog as primary elements
-   Subtle lighting effects
-   Emotional tone over detail

### Elements

-   Rain layers, snow, fog
-   Lantern glow, window light
-   Umbrellas, bridges, bare trees

### Special Feature

-   Atmospheric overlays

### Prompts

-   "Rain at dusk"
-   "A quiet street in snow"
-   "Light in the distance"

### Monetization

High-value premium pack

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Kacho-e: Birds & Flowers

### Concept

Close-up nature compositions featuring birds and plants.

### Key Characteristics

-   Intimate compositions
-   Interaction between elements
-   Organic curves

### Elements

-   Birds (crane, sparrow, heron)
-   Blossoms (cherry, plum)
-   Iris, chrysanthemum
-   Branch arcs

### Prompts

-   "A bird in early spring"
-   "Blossoms and stillness"
-   "A moment before flight"

### Monetization

Broad appeal premium pack

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Wabi-Sabi Minimalism

### Concept

Extreme minimalism focused on imperfection and space.

### Key Characteristics

-   Sparse compositions
-   Rough textures
-   Asymmetry and imbalance

### Elements

-   Single branches, stones, abstract forms
-   Texture overlays (ink bleed, rough edges)

### Special Feature

-   Imperfection controls (edge roughness, ink variation)

### Prompts

-   "One line, one space"
-   "Almost empty"
-   "An unfinished feeling"

### Monetization

Premium niche pack

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Strategic Value

These packs transform Mokuri into a system of creative styles:

-   Core → Landscapes
-   Moribana → Structured minimalism
-   Shin-hanga → Atmosphere
-   Kacho-e → Nature interaction
-   Wabi-sabi → Abstract minimalism

### Outcome

-   Increased retention
-   Clear monetization pathways
-   Stronger brand identity
